## What it is

A simple operations app for a **multi-branch laundry business**. Today the owner checks
daily operations manually over WhatsApp; the app replaces that scattered, chat-based
oversight with one structured place where every branch reports in each day.

Working title: **Laundry Ops** (name not yet confirmed).

## The problem

- The business runs **several branches** (beberapa cabang).
- Daily operations are still checked **manually via WhatsApp** — updates, photos, and
  problems are scattered across chats.
- As branches grow, the owner becomes the single human "system" tracking everything,
  which does not scale and lets issues slip through until they become customer
  complaints.

## The solution

A lightweight daily-operations app that gives the owner visibility across all branches
in one place. Core capabilities the founder named:

1. **Branch daily checklist** — each branch completes a daily operational checklist.
2. **Laundry status tracking** — track the status of laundry orders / loads.
3. **Photo proof** — staff attach photo evidence to checklist items / status updates.
4. **Daily problem reports** — staff log problems each day; the owner sees them centrally.

## Who uses it

- **Branch staff / operators** — complete the daily checklist, update laundry status,
  upload photo proof, and report problems from their branch.
- **Owner / operations manager** — monitors all branches in one dashboard, reviews photo
  evidence, and acts on reported problems early.

## Positioning & tone

- Practical, no-frills operational tool for an Indonesian small business (UKM).
- Founder-facing language is Indonesian; the product favors **simplicity over feature
  breadth** — "aplikasi sederhana" was an explicit requirement.
- Value is **operational visibility and consistency across branches**, enabling the owner
  to catch problems early and expand without losing control of quality.

## Strategic principles

- Keep it simple: one daily flow per branch, one overview for the owner.
- Photo evidence and a clear daily record are what make remote oversight trustworthy.
- The product should make adding a new branch a low-stress, repeatable event.
